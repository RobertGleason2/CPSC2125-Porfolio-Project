Making a Sandwich

Today we are going to be making a sandwitch today

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Letuce
  • Cheese
  • Bread

Instructions

  1. First you would need to start with grilling the chicken in a pan. The chicken can be seasoned with any seasoning you like.
  2. Toast the bread in a toaster until it's golden brown.
  3. Add the chicken to the break with leduce and cheese.
